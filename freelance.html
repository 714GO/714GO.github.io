Overview

Before stepping into institutional and agency roles, I spent over three years building an independent digital and brand strategy practice. Working across diverse industries — climate tech database, ERP provider, print agency, marketing consulting firm, hair salon, to professional services and e-commerce — I developed the self-directed discipline, client ownership, and strategic breadth that now underpins everything I do.

Freelance work demands a different kind of accountability: no hierarchy to fall back on, no team to absorb the gap. Every audit, every strategy, every client relationship was mine end-to-end. That foundation — of deep ownership, rigorous analysis, and clear communication — is what I brought into institutional settings and what continues to shape my professional approach.
